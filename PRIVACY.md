# NexisOS Privacy Policy

NexisOS is a free and open-source Linux distribution. This project is maintained by an independent developer and is currently under **active development**.

## Project Status

NexisOS is in **early development and not yet in pre-alpha release**. Core architecture is still being designed, and no official binaries or installer ISOs are currently available. All features are experimental, and the software is intended for testing, evaluation, and development purposes only. Users should not expect a production-ready system at this stage.

## Data Collection

- NexisOS **does not collect, transmit, process, or store personal data** from end users.  
- No telemetry, analytics, or monitoring is enabled by default.  
- Any data collection originates only from third-party software installed by the user, not by NexisOS itself.

## Age Verification & Identity Policy

NexisOS will comply with **reasonable, legally mandated age-assurance or child-protection requirements**, provided they can be implemented **without introducing security risks or invasive privacy practices**.

NexisOS will **never** implement:

- Identity verification mechanisms that expose personally identifiable information (PII) to external services.  
- Tracking, centralized authentication, or persistent device identifiers solely for regulatory compliance.  
- Any mechanism that could weaken system security or compromise user privacy.

Where age-assurance features are required, implementations will aim to:

- Minimize or avoid data collection entirely.  
- Keep verification **local to the device** whenever possible.  
- Protect **user anonymity and system security**.

Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with laws in their jurisdiction. This policy reflects the project’s commitment to **open-source principles, security, and privacy**, while acknowledging the evolving legal landscape.

## User Responsibility

- Users are responsible for understanding any privacy or data collection implications of third-party software they install.  
- For full legal and compliance information, see [DISCLAIMER.md](./DISCLAIMER.md).

## Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the project evolves. Users are encouraged to review it periodically.
